What is this?

'No Eye For Design' is a website that displays the most horrifying webpages while discussing UX/UI rules. Every webpage will have its own theme of bad design, for instance, our Introduction page theme is 'Insane'! The goal of this site is to be a reference of what NOT to do when designing a website. Feel free to donate to @rosawashere on venmo to show your support for bad design, thank you and enjoy.
