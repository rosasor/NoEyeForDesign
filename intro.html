What is UX?

User experience is the overall experience of a person using a product such as a website or a computer application, especially in terms of how easy or pleasing it is to use, just like this website. UX is how a user interacts with and experiences a product, system, or service and it includes a person's perceptions of utility, ease of use, and efficiency. User experience (UX) design is the process design teams use to create products that provide meaningful and relevant experiences to users.

What is UI?

User Interface is the point of interaction between humans and computers. User interface (UI) design is the process designers use to build interfaces in software or computerized devices, with a focus on maximizing usability and the user. It is the process of designing how these interfaces look and behave.
